# 🤖 AI vs Human Challenge (The Reality Check)

## 🎯 The Core Concept
The internet is flooded with "How to get rich using AI" videos. However, almost nobody actually tests these theories in real life. In this content format, the creator asks an AI (like ChatGPT or Claude) for a specific, ultra-low-budget business model (e.g., *“I have a 500 BDT budget, give me a business plan to make 100 BDT daily profit in Rajshahi”*). 

The creator then takes that money, goes out into the real world, and attempts to execute the AI's exact roadmap step-by-step to see if it actually works or if it's just pure hallucination.

## 🎬 Why This Will Go Viral
*   **Authentic Hustle:** Audiences love real-world social experiments, struggles, and raw execution over scripted content.
*   **The Reality Check:** It provides an honest, unbiased review of AI capabilities, building massive trust with your viewers.
*   **High Engagement:** Viewers will actively debate in the comments and suggest crazier prompts for the next episodes.

## 🛠 Step-by-Step Execution Guide

### Phase 1: The Prompting & Strategy
*   Give the AI highly specific, local constraints (budget, location, resources).
*   Document the exact blueprint, timeline, and rules given by the AI.

### Phase 2: The Human Expert Audit (The Twist)
*   Before hitting the streets, take the AI's plan to a local shopkeeper, businessman, or expert in that field.
*   Show them the plan and record their reaction. Let them point out the flaws in the AI's logic.

### Phase 3: The Ground Reality Test
*   Go out and buy raw materials, execute the marketing, and try to make sales exactly how the AI recommended.
*   Film everything—the rejections, the awkward moments, and the unexpected challenges.

### Phase 4: The Final Verdict
*   Break down the math transparently: How much was spent? How much was earned?
*   Give the AI a final rating based on accuracy, practicality, and profitability.
